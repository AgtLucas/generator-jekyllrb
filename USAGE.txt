Description:
    Creates a Jekyll site and wires watch, test, and build tasks for Grunt.

Example:
    yo jekyll

    RWRW TO EDIT
    This will create:
        Gruntfile.js
        component.json

        app/index.html
        app/scripts/your-app-name-here.js
        app/scripts/controllers/main.js
        app/components/angular/angular.js
        app/styles/main.css
        app/views/main.html

        test/lib/angular-mocks.js
        test/spec/controllers/main.js
